White Beans & Greans

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 large bunch kale or other greens
  • 2 cans (total 30-32 oz) white beans, drained and rinsed
  • 3 cups vegetable stock
  • 1 medium lemon, zest & juice divided
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Saute the onion until transparent (6 to 7 minutes).
  3. Add garlic, red pepper flakes. Cook for 1 more minute.
  4. Add greens and cook for a few minutes until slightly wilted.
  5. Add beans and mash about half of them. (Use a potato masher or the back of a fork)
  6. Add stock, lemon zest, bay leaf, thyme, salt, pepper.
  7. Simmer uncovered until reduced and thickened (~10 minutes).
  8. Take off heat, add lemon juice, adjust seasoning as desired.

Chickpea Nuggets

Ingredients

  • 1 can (total 15-16 oz) chickpeas, drained
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 - 1/2 cup vegan parmesean
  • 2 tbsp dry parsley
  • 3 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp canola oil (for frying)

Instructions

  1. Mash chickpeas well or blend with a food processor.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and mix.
  3. Heat oil in large skillet.
  4. Make nugget/patty shapes of chickpea mixture.
  5. Shallow fry nuggets. Turning until both sides are golden brown. Be careful, they are fragile.
  6. Cool and de-oil on paper town.
